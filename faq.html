Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pantry Pal Ai?

Pantry Pal Ai is a free tool that generates recipe ideas based on the ingredients you have on hand.

How do I use it?

Simply select or type in your available ingredients, click 'Get Recipes', and Pantry Pal Ai will suggest AI-generated recipes using what you have.

Is it really free?

Yes — it’s completely free to use!

Can I use Pantry Pal Ai on my phone?

Absolutely — Pantry Pal Ai is mobile-friendly and works smoothly on any smartphone or tablet browser.

Does Pantry Pal Ai suggest healthy or vegan recipes?

While Pantry Pal Ai isn’t limited to vegan or health-specific recipes, you can enter plant-based or healthy ingredients to get recipes that suit your preferences.

What types of ingredients can I use with Pantry Pal Ai?

You can use any ingredient — from pantry staples like rice, beans, and spices to fresh produce, proteins, and condiments. Pantry Pal Ai turns them into instant recipe ideas.

Is Pantry Pal Ai safe and private?

Yes. Pantry Pal Ai doesn’t collect personal data or require any sign-ups. You can use it anonymously and safely.
