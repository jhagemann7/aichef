Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pantry Pal Ai?
Pantry Pal Ai is a free tool that generates recipe ideas based on the ingredients you have on hand.
How do I use it?
Simply select or type in your available ingredients, click 'Get Recipes', and Pantry Pal Ai will suggest AI-generated recipes using what you have.
Is it really free?
Yes — it’s completely free to use!
